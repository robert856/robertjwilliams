Professional Summary

Visionary Product Development Executive with 35+ years of expertise in luxury furniture, cabinetry, kitchen & bath, and home décor. Proven record driving product lines from concept to market for premium brands and high-profile estate licensors including Biltmore Estate, Hearst Castle, and Monet Estate. Deep expertise in international sourcing, factory qualification across Asia, and industrial coatings development in collaboration with Sherwin-Williams and AkzoNobel. Equally effective as a solo creative principal and cross-functional team leader — combining executive business acumen with advanced technical fluency in 3D visualization, CAD, and rendering. Complementary background as a self-taught set designer and scenic artist, with six productions for Heritage Christian Theatre.
