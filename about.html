For over 25 years, I have worked as a product designer and product development executive. I have created products and collections for luxury brands, managing design teams and processes to bring exceptional products from concept to market and through first production runs.

I never thought my profession would bleed into my family life. My wife and kids were involved in community theatre, and eventually I started helping out. What began as occasional assistance quickly turned into designing entire sets for shows. This experience sparked a new passion in me: designing immersive storytelling experiences through set design and set choreography. I am now excited to blend my design expertise with the art of theatre.
